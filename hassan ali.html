The Greenhouse Effect

An overview of how the Earth's atmosphere traps heat and its impact on climate change.


1. Transition to Renewable Energy

Switching to renewable energy sources, such as solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal power, is one of the most effective solutions to reduce greenhouse gas emissions. Unlike fossil fuels, renewables do not release harmful carbon dioxide into the atmosphere.

2. Improve Energy Efficiency

Improving energy efficiency in homes, buildings, and industries reduces the overall energy demand, thus lowering emissions. This can be achieved through better insulation, efficient appliances, and advanced technologies.

4. Implement Carbon Pricing

Carbon pricing, such as carbon taxes or cap-and-trade systems, incentivizes businesses to reduce their carbon emissions. This creates a financial motivation to transition to cleaner energy sources and more efficient practices.
