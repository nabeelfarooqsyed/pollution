Introduction to Water Pollution

Understanding the Impact and What You Can Do


Deforestation

refers to the large-scale clearing, removal, or destruction of forests, often to make way for agricultural activities, urban development, or other human needs. This process leads to a reduction in the number of trees and forested areas, which play a vital role in maintaining ecological balance

Causes of the deforestation

  1. Agricultural Expansion
  2. Logging
  3. Urbanization
  4. maining
  5. Infrastructure development

Impacts of deforestation

Deforestation destroys the natural habitats of many species, leading to a loss of biodiversity. Species that depend on forests for food, shelter, and breeding may face extinction if they cannot adapt or migrate.
