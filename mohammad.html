Introduction to Air Pollution

know impact and causes of air pollution


What is the air pollution?

Air pollution refers to the presence of harmful substances in the atmosphere that can negatively impact human health, ecosystems, and the climate. These substances can include gases, liquids, and solid particles that are release into the air through both natural processes and human activities. .

Causes of the air pollution

Air pollution is caused by the release of harmful substances into the atmosphere, which can have negative effects on both human health and the environment.

Impacts of the Air pollution

Some air pollutants, like chlorofluorocarbons (CFCs), contribute to the depletion of the ozone layer, which protects the Earth from harmful ultraviolet (UV) radiation. Increased UV radiation can lead to higher rates of skin cancer, cataracts, and harm to ecosystems.
