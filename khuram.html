What is Land Pollution?

Land pollution refers to the degradation of the earthâ€™s surface caused by human activity and the misuse of natural resources. It is primarily caused by the dumping of waste, industrial activities, and deforestation.

Causes of Land Pollution

  • Improper waste disposal
  • Deforestation
  • Industrial waste
  • Urbanization and land development

Effects of Land Pollution

Land pollution harms the environment in various ways. It leads to the destruction of natural habitats, health problems, and contributes to the depletion of soil quality.
